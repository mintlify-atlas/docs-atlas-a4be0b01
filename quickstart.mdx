Skillhouse is live at [skillhouse-sable.vercel.app](https://skillhouse-sable.vercel.app). No local setup is required to start using the platform. Follow the steps below to create an account and complete your first action as either a freelancer or a client.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the platform">
    Open [https://skillhouse-sable.vercel.app](https://skillhouse-sable.vercel.app) in your browser. You will land on the homepage, which includes a job category browser, testimonials, and the smart bot assistant for common questions.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create an account">
    Click **Sign up** and choose how you want to register:

    - **Email and password** — enter your name, email, and a secure password
    - **Google OAuth** — click **Continue with Google** to sign in with your Google account

    After entering your details, you will be prompted to select your role on the platform.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select your role">
    Choose one of two roles:

    - **Freelancer** — you will offer services, apply to jobs, and receive payments
    - **Client** — you will post jobs, hire freelancers, and manage contracts

    <Info>
      Your role determines which dashboard and features you have access to. You cannot switch roles after selection without creating a new account.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify your email with OTP">
    After registration, Skillhouse sends a one-time password (OTP) to your email address. Enter the OTP on the verification screen to activate your account. OTPs expire after a short window — if yours expires, request a new one from the same screen.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete your profile">
    Before taking any action on the platform, fill out your profile. The fields differ by role.

    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="As a freelancer">
        - **Profile photo** — uploaded and stored via Cloudinary
        - **Bio** — a short description of your background and expertise
        - **Skills** — select from the platform's skill taxonomy
        - **Hourly rate** — your default rate, visible to clients browsing profiles
        - **Portfolio** — optional work samples (images or videos)
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="As a client">
        - **Profile photo** — uploaded and stored via Cloudinary
        - **Bio** — a short description of your company or project needs
        - **Company information** — optional, displayed on your public profile
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## What to do next

Once your profile is complete, your path on the platform diverges based on your role.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="As a freelancer">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Browse open jobs">
        Go to the **Jobs** section from your dashboard. You can filter by category, required skills, and budget range. Each listing shows the job description, required skills, and the client's budget.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Submit a proposal">
        On any job listing, click **Apply**. Write a cover letter explaining your approach, set your proposed rate, and submit. You can track all submitted proposals under **Applied jobs**.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Contract and escrow">
        When a client accepts your proposal, a contract is created automatically. The client funds the escrow via Stripe at this point — you can see the contract status in your **Contracts** dashboard.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Deliver your work">
        Complete the work and mark the contract as delivered. The client reviews your submission and, if satisfied, approves the payment release.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Receive payment">
        Once the client approves and the admin confirms, the funds are released to your Skillhouse wallet. View your earnings and transaction history under **Earnings**.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="As a client">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Post a job">
        From your dashboard, click **Post a job**. Fill in:

        - **Title** — a concise name for the work
        - **Description** — detailed requirements and deliverables
        - **Budget** — the amount you are willing to pay
        - **Required skills** — used to match your job to relevant freelancers

        Your job will appear on the public job board immediately after posting.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Review proposals">
        Freelancers will start submitting proposals. Review each one from your dashboard — you can view the freelancer's profile, portfolio, and proposed rate before deciding.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Accept a proposal and fund escrow">
        When you find the right freelancer, accept their proposal. A contract is created and you will be redirected to Stripe to pay the agreed amount. Your payment is held in escrow until you approve the final delivery.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Review and approve delivery">
        When the freelancer marks work as delivered, review the submission and approve it if you are satisfied. Your approval triggers the admin review and final payment release.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Leave a review">
        After the contract closes, leave a rating and written review for the freelancer. Reviews help other clients make informed hiring decisions and help freelancers build their reputation on the platform.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Smart bot assistant

The Skillhouse homepage includes a built-in chat bot that can answer common questions about how the platform works — including how escrow functions, how to reset your password, and how to navigate contracts. Look for the chat icon in the bottom-right corner of the homepage.

<Tip>
  If you get stuck at any point in the onboarding flow, the bot assistant can walk you through the most common issues without needing to contact support.
</Tip>
